Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before enrolling

What is the DGCA Remote Pilot Certificate (RPC)? +
The Remote Pilot Certificate (RPC) is the official government license issued by DGCA that authorizes you to legally fly drones commercially in India. It is mandatory for operating medium and large category drones for commercial purposes. .
How long is the DGCA RPC valid? +
The Remote Pilot Certificate (RPC) is valid for 10 years from the date of issue. After 10 years, you will need to renew it. There is no annual renewal required during the validity period.
Do I need prior drone experience? +
Absolutely not! Our 5-day program is designed for complete beginners. We start from drone components and build up to advanced flight maneuvers. Over 60% of our students have zero prior flying experience.
What is the fee for the course? +
Course fees vary based on the category (small/medium/large drone license). Contact us for the latest pricing and available EMI options. We also offer group discounts for 3+ enrollments from the same organization.
What drones will I fly during training? +
You'll get hands-on experience with multiple drone types including DJI multirotors, agriculture spray drones, and mapping drones with cameras. The specific fleet depends on the category of license you're pursuing.
Can I start my own drone business after the course? +
Absolutely! We also provide entrepreneurship mentoring to help you set up your drone service business. From drone procurement to pricing strategy and finding your first clients — we guide you through it all.
Do you provide job placement after the course? +
Yes! We have a dedicated placement cell with 200+ industry partners across agriculture, survey, filmmaking, and infrastructure sectors. We help with resume building, interview preparation, and direct company referrals.
